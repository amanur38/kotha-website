What is Kotha?

"Every Bangladeshi Deserves a
Voice and Opportunity—
Kotha Delivers"

Kotha is Bangladesh’s own fully homegrown social and marketplace platform, designed to reflect our culture, values, and ambitions. It’s your online community center, social media, classroom, and marketplace all rolled into one secure, trusted environment.

Here, “Kotha” means more than conversation—it’s a promise: Our mission is simple but powerful: to give every Bangladeshi a voice and help them seize opportunities—at home and beyond.
